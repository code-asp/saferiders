The Strategy

Practical, immediate, and lasting behavioral change.

Smart Education

Engagement over boring rules. Our interactive onboarding ensures safety is top-of-mind before the motor even starts.

  • Mandatory engagement walkthroughs
  • Contextual safety pop-ups
  • Visual rule reinforcement

In-App Nudges

Leveraging behavioral science to reward responsible riding while making rule-breaking harder to ignore.

  • Photo-verified parking zones
  • Performance-based ride credits
  • Accountability for repeated misuse

Campus Synergy

Using universities as ideal pilots. We build a culture of safety through peer-to-peer influence and ambassadors.

  • Student ambassador programs
  • Campus-wide safety challenges
  • Localized pilot programs
