The Strategy

Our framework addresses the urban safety crisis through practical, tech-driven behavioral change.

Smart Education

Replacing boring rules with engagement-first onboarding to ensure riders are informed before the motor starts.

  • Mandatory safety walkthroughs
  • Contextual smart notifications
  • Visual rule reinforcement

In-App Nudges

Leveraging behavioral science to reward responsible riding while making rule-breaking harder to ignore.

  • Photo-verified parking zones
  • Performance ride credits
  • Misuse restriction triggers

Campus Synergy

Utilizing universities as ideal testing grounds for a culture of safety through peer influence.

  • Student Ambassador programs
  • On-campus safety challenges
  • Collaborative local outreach
